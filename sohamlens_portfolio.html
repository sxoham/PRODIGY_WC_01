About Me

Hi, I’m Soham — the eye behind SohamLens. I’m a passionate photographer based in India, dedicated to turning fleeting moments into timeless visual memories.

From candid street shots to grand wedding frames, I believe every picture has a story. My journey began with a simple phone camera and grew into a full-fledged creative pursuit.

Photography, to me, is not just about technique; it’s about connection — with people, with light, and with the moment itself.

Let’s create something beautiful together.
